# Shopify_Dashboard
This project presents an interactive sales dashboard built using Power BI to analyze Shopify store data. The dataset was sourced from Microsoft Excel and transformed to generate meaningful business insights.
The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of key performance metrics such as total sales, revenue trends, top-selling products, and customer purchasing behavior. Basic DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) queries were used to create calculated measures and enhance data analysis capabilities.

● Key Features:
1. Sales performance analysis (daily, monthly trends)
2. Top products and category insights
3. Customer behavior and purchase patterns
3. Interactive filters and slicers for dynamic exploration
 
● Tools & Technologies Used:
1. Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
2. Microsoft Excel (Data Source)
3. Basic DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

● Dataset Used : 

• Shopify Data.

● Questions(KPIs) :

• Net Sales KPIs

 1. What is the **total net sale** overtime?
 2. What is the percentage contribution of each **payment gateway** to net sales?
 3. Which **product category contributes the most** to Net Sales?
 4. Which **region/province/city generates the highest** net sales?

• Total Quantity KPIs

 1. What is the **total quantity** sold over time?
 2. Which **payment method** has the highest number of items sold?
 3. Which **product type** has the highest sales volume?
 4. Which **region/cities/** have the highest quantity sold?

• Total Customers KPIs

 1.How many **customers were acquired** over time?
 2.Which **payment method** is most used by customers?
 3.Which **product categories** attract the most customers?
 4.Which **regions** have the highest number of customers?

• Repeated Customers KPIs

 1.How does the **number of repeated customers** change over time?
 2.Which **payment methods** are preferred by repeat customers?
 3.Which **product categories** have the highest repeat purchase rate?
 4.Which **regions** have the highest number of repeat customers?


 ● Project Insights 
 
  • Net sales shows **clear fluctuations over time**, indicating the presence of seasonal demand patterns and possible promotional impacts.
  
  •A majority of Net sales is driven by Digital payment methods, with **Shopify payments and PayPal contributing the largest share**, alternate payment options           indicates lower customer adoption.
  
  •Categories such as **running, tennis and walking shoes contribute significantly** to both revenue and sales volume, making them key business drivers.
  
  •**Underperforming categories** highlight opportunities for product optimization, **pricing adjustment**, or targeted marketing.
  
  •High-performing regions contribute consistently across all KPIs, while **some regions show untapped growth potential**.


● Recommendation 

  •**Focus on high-performing products and regions** to maximize revenue.
  
  •**Identify and improve low-performing products and regions** through targeted marketing, pricing strategies, and better positioning.
  
  •**Enhance customer retention** using loyalty programs and personalized campaigns.
  
  •**Promote underutilized payment methods** to improve customer convinience.
  
  •**Use sales trends** for effective demand forecasting and inventory planning.


● Conclusion 

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of **sales performance**, **customer behaviour**, and **regional trends**, enabling data-driven decision-making. It identifies key revenue drivers as well as **underperforming areas that require attention**.
